About Clean & Sweep

Clean & Sweep

Clean & Sweep is committed to providing quality services to its clients in the Northwest Chicago area. Trust and satisfaction are our top priorities, which is why we always deliver on our promises. With years of experience in residential cleaning, we have honed our skills to provide exceptional service. Our dedicated team comprises insured and bonded staff who undergo comprehensive training to ensure unparalleled results. By prioritizing the use of environmentally friendly products, equipment, and systems, we have established ourselves as industry leaders in our area. Not only does this approach benefit the environment, but it also creates healthier environments for our valued clients.
