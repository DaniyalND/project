Regular Cleans

Kitchen
  • Wipe down kitchen appliances (stove, microwave, refrigerator)
  • Clean countertops
  • Disinfect the sink
  • Wash dishes or load them into the dishwasher
  • Empty trash bin
  • Clean the exterior of all appliances
  • Wipe down and disinfect light switches and doorknobs
  • Sweep or vacuum the floor
  • Mop the floor
Bathroom
  • Clean and disinfect the sink
  • Scrub and sanitize the toilet
  • Clean the shower and bathtub
  • Wipe down bathroom surfaces
  • Clean mirrors
  • Empty trash bin
  • Wipe down and disinfect light switches and doorknobs
  • Sweep or vacuum the floor
  • Mop the floor
Bedroom
  • Make the bed
  • Dust and wipe down all surfaces, including dressers and nightstands
  • Vacuum carpets or sweep floors
  • Clean windows and mirrors
  • Empty the trash bin
Living Areas
  • Dust all surfaces, including shelves, tables, and entertainment centers
  • Wipe down furniture
  • Vacuum carpets or sweep floors
  • Clean windows and mirrors
  • Organize and declutter
  • Vacuum upholstery if needed
